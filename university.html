About Us

Established with the Law No. 7141 published in the Official Gazette dated May 18, 2018 and issued 30425, Eskişehir Technical University consists of 5 Faculties, 2 Vocational Schools, 1 School and 3 Institutions and 6 Research and Application Centers in 3 campuses located in the center of Eskişehir, which is a city of science, culture and youth. Eskişehir Technical University works to prepare its students for the future as the most equipped and competitive individuals in creative and dynamic educational environments, thanks to its competent teaching staff in all its units with strong infrastructure, where education and research activities are carried out. The operation of the international Hasan Polatkan Airport, located in the 2 Eylül Campus of our university, is carried out by our Faculty of Aeronautics and Space Sciences. There are international flights to Hasan Polatkan Airport operated via different airline companies on various days of the week.

Numbers of Students and Staff

Eskişehir Technical University, which has a total of 10,000 students, 1,000 of whom are graduate students, and 1,000 academic staff, 500 of whom are faculty members, continues its activities in 3 campuses, 5 faculties, 2 vocational schools, 1 school and 3 institutes.

Administrative Units

The Rectorate is the executive branch of our institution, responsible for strategic decision-making and overall management. It ensures that all university activities align with our mission and vision.

Administrative Affairs handles the essential functions that keep our institution running smoothly. This includes finance, human resources, facilities management, and day-to-day operations.

Student Affairs is dedicated to supporting student success both in and outside the classroom. This department oversees housing, student activities, counseling services, and career development.

International Relations fosters global partnerships and opportunities. This department coordinates international programs, student and faculty exchanges, and develops relationships with institutions around the world.

Research and Development drives our commitment to innovation and scholarly excellence. This department supports faculty and student research initiatives and manages collaborations with industry and other academic entities.
